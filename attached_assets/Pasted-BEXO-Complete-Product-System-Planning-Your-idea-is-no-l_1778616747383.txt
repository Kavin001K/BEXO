BEXO — Complete Product & System Planning
Your idea is no longer just a portfolio app.
It is becoming an AI-powered identity + portfolio operating system for students and professionals.
The onboarding flow must feel:
Fast
Premium
Intelligent
Human
Trustworthy
The biggest mistake would be making it feel like a boring form-filling app.
So the architecture should be:
“Minimal questions → AI helps → User reviews → Portfolio instantly generated.”
